Our story
Built by students who were tired of messy group chats.

connectr+ started as a small side project to replace chaotic WhatsApp threads, random spreadsheets, and overlapping Instagram stories. We wanted one calm, beautiful place where students could actually find what they're looking for – without spam or scams.

Designed for campus Trust-first Zero bidding wars
Our mission
Make campus trading circular, local, and low-waste.

Our mission is to keep great stuff in circulation and out of landfills. connectr+ helps students pass on books, tech, furniture and tickets so they live a second (or third) life on campus — saving money, cutting waste, and shrinking everyone’s footprint in the process.

  • Give pre-loved items a new home before anyone buys something new.
  • Keep money and materials circulating within your campus community.
  • Enjoy a premium, safe experience that quietly makes campus life more sustainable.
